Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are never sent to any server.

Is FileFlux really free with no limits?

Yes! FileFlux is completely free with no file size limits (beyond your browser's memory), no daily caps, and no watermarks on outputs.

How does Image to Text (OCR) work?

We use Tesseract.js, a powerful open-source OCR engine that runs directly in your browser. It supports English, Hindi, French, German, Spanish and more.

What image formats are supported?

FileFlux supports PNG, JPG/JPEG, WebP, and BMP for most tools. PDF support includes reading and generating multi-page documents.

Can I convert multiple images to PDF?

Yes! The Image → PDF tool accepts multiple files. You can drag and drop or select them all at once, and they'll be merged into a single PDF in order.

Does FileFlux work on mobile?

Absolutely. FileFlux is fully responsive and optimized for touch devices. All features work on iOS and Android browsers.
